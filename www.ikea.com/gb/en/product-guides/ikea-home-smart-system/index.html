See, hear, feel. Imagine your home connected, just like your senses.

Imagine a home – a smart home – that automates the little tasks you do every morning. Like raising the blinds or playing your favourite playlist. Connected devices don’t just make life easier, they make it better, and it can all start at IKEA. 

 

What is a smart home?

A smart home is a home containing connected devices – products that can be controlled via the internet from your smartphone, tablet, or through a virtual assistant.

Take a look at our SYMFONISK collection, for example, built and designed in collaboration with Sonos for home automation. The Wi-Fi-enabled lamp, with built-in speakers, combines high quality sound with soft lighting, meaning one less cord, one less power socket, and one less device.

 

What is a smart home device?

Devices that are connected to the internet and controlled through a smartphone or by a virtual assistant, are ‘smart’ devices. They’re designed to make your life easier, either by automating common tasks or giving you access to more information.

 

How do you make a smart home?

Getting started with smart home technology is simple, but the most important thing you’ll need is good Wi-Fi signal, as all devices need to be connected to the internet to operate. And then it’s simply a case of collecting and installing the devices that will make your life easier.
