Delivery

Most of our products are flat packed and designed in such a way that they can easily fit in your car. However, if you prefer to have your purchase delivered, you can choose among our range of delivery options.

Shopping at IKEA.com

If you’re placing an order online, we offer the following delivery methods:

Smaller delivery – £3.95*

Smaller deliveries are fulfilled by DPD Monday-Sunday at a cost of £3.95, £9.95 or £19.95 depending on the size of your order.

*Your delivery cost will be calculated in the shopping bag based on your chosen products.

Larger delivery – £39

Larger two man deliveries are fulfilled by our approved suppliers, Monday-Sunday depending on your postcode. This is for larger orders or any product that we deem unsuitable for parcel delivery.

For further information, please view our FAQ

Shopping at your local IKEA store

When visiting our stores, you have different options to have your purchase delivered to your home. And, we don’t just stop at your front door, we deliver all the way to your room of choice.

Truck delivery – £29
(within store area)*

Truck delivery – £39
(outside of store area)

Same/next day delivery is subject to availability at our local transport partner.

*Please check with our friendly Virtual Assistant or enquire in-store for the postcodes included in the store delivery area.

Picking with delivery 3 days – £39

Picking with delivery same day/next day

Within the store delivery area postcodes: £43

Outside of the store delivery postcode area: £53

Simply place your order with one of our co-workers in-store and they will arrange the picking with delivery for you.

*Please check with our friendly Virtual Assistant or enquire in-store for the postcodes included in the store delivery area.
